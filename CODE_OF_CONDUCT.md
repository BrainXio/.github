# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We — contributors, maintainers, and curious minds — pledge to make BrainXio a welcoming space where everyone feels free to question, create, and connect without fear or judgment.

We value curiosity over certainty, honesty over comfort, and support over control. This community thrives when we lift each other up, laugh at absurdities, and explore together with open hearts.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps us grow:
- Asking "why" with genuine interest
- Giving and receiving feedback with kindness and clarity
- Celebrating different perspectives and experiences
- Owning mistakes and learning from them
- Choosing empathy even when it's hard

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or derision
- Publishing private information without consent
- Behavior that silences questions or suppresses freedom

## Enforcement

Community stewards will enforce this Code gently but firmly. Violations may lead to warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal, depending on impact.

Report issues privately to github@brainxio.org — we'll handle them respectfully and promptly.

## Scope & Attribution

This Code applies across all BrainXio spaces and when representing the community publicly.
